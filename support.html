Frequently asked questions

Is my data private?
Yes. Everything you enter stays on your device. There are no accounts and no analytics. See our Privacy Policy.
What does Pro unlock?
Managers Playbook Pro is a one-time purchase (no subscription) that unlocks the AI assistant chat, the 1:1 recorder, inbox drafting, and calendar export, and removes the free daily AI limit.
How does the AI work without an account?
The assistant runs on-device using Apple Intelligence or a small model you can download. You can optionally use your own free Google Gemini key instead. Nothing is sent anywhere unless you set up Gemini.
I bought Pro on another device — how do I restore it?
Go to Settings ▸ Pro ▸ Restore Purchase.
How do I export or delete my data?
Open Settings ▸ Data. You can export everything as a file or reset all data at any time.
Which devices are supported?
iPhone and iPad running iOS/iPadOS 17 or later. The downloadable local model and Apple Intelligence require a compatible device.
